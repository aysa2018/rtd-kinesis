Arena
-----

The Arena is a dedicated space for motion experimentation.

**Specifications**

- Size: 17m × 6.4m × 8m
- Adaptable truss structure with safety nets
- Adjustable lighting (color and intensity)
- Protective floor mattresses

**Equipped with**

- High-accuracy position tracking system
- 2 kW sound system
- Projection system
- High-speed ethernet and wireless network

**Use cases**

- Indoor flight testing
- Mobile manipulation
- Multi-robot experiments
- Biomechanics and motion studies


Workspace
---------

The Workspace is a flexible environment for development and experimentation.

**Features**

- Workstations for coding and data processing
- High-speed local network
- Adjustable lighting
- Nearby benches with tools and test equipment
- GPU unit for high-performance computation
- Safe charging station for LiPo batteries


Equipment Overview
------------------

The lab supports motion-centric research with a comprehensive toolkit.

**Robotics & Platforms**

- Humanoids, quadrupeds, wheeled robots
- Autonomous car platform
- Aerial drones (including RTK-enabled systems)
- Underwater ROVs

**Mapping & Scanning**

- Terrestrial LiDAR
- SLAM-based mapping (UAV, handheld, backpack)
- Object and environment scanners

**Imaging & Sensors**

- Hyperspectral imaging
- Thermal and visual zoom cameras
- Event-based vision systems
- Acoustic imaging

**Communication & Testing**

- Mesh radio networks
- Bench testing equipment


Applications and Research
-------------------------

**Biomechanical**

- Gait analysis and rehabilitation
- Posture and motor control
- Sports performance analysis
- Animal biomechanics

**Engineering**

- Robotics (UAVs, ROVs, UGVs, humanoids)
- Localization and trajectory planning
- Human-robot interaction (HRI)
- Teleoperation and control systems

**Reality Capture & Digital Twins**

- SLAM mapping
- LiDAR and photogrammetry
- Asset documentation and change detection

**Perception & Sensing**

- Thermal imaging
- Hyperspectral analysis
- Event-based vision
- Acoustic diagnostics

**Artificial Intelligence**

- Dataset curation and labeling
- Deep learning and reinforcement learning
- Large language models for robotics and HRI

**Networking & Edge Computing**

- High-bandwidth mesh communication
- Remote monitoring and telemetry

**Design & Prototyping**

- 3D scanning and reverse engineering
- Assembly testing and validation

**Marine Applications**

- Underwater monitoring and surveys

**Entertainment**

- Game development (animation, effects)
- Film production (character and motion animation)
- Sound and motion research
